Important Details

What You Need to Know

Clear, straightforward information to make your rental experience smooth and enjoyable.

💳

Payment Policy

Full payment is required before departure or pickup. We accept all major payment methods. No hidden fees — the price you see is the price you pay.

📝

Waiver Requirement

All self-operated rentals require a signed waiver before departure. This covers liability and ensures you understand the vessel and safety procedures. Captain-assisted rentals do not require a waiver.

👨‍✈️

Add a Captain

A captain can be added to any rental for an additional fee. This is recommended for unfamiliar waters, larger groups, or anyone who simply wants to relax and enjoy the ride.

🔧

Well-Maintained Fleet

Every vessel in our fleet undergoes regular maintenance and safety inspections. You will receive a thorough orientation before departure, including safety equipment and navigation basics.

Easy Process

How to Rent

Renting a boat is simple and straightforward.

1

Choose Your Vessel

Contact us with your preferred date, group size, and whether you want to self-operate or add a captain.

2

Confirm & Pay

We will confirm availability and send you the details. Full payment secures your booking.

3

Sign & Brief

Sign the waiver (self-operated only), receive your orientation, and get familiar with the vessel.

4

Set Sail

Head out on the water and enjoy the freedom of the Bahamas. Return at the agreed time.
