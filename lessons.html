Captain License Practical Exam Support

Preparing for your captain's license? Our experienced, certified captains provide focused practical exam preparation to help you pass with confidence. We cover all the hands-on skills and scenarios you will face during the practical examination.

  • Focused practical exam preparation
  • Real-world scenario training
  • Docking, undocking, and close-quarters maneuvering
  • Man overboard drills and emergency procedures
  • Rules of the road and navigation exercises
  • Personalized feedback and coaching
