Policies

What You Need to Know

Clear, straightforward policies designed to protect you and ensure a smooth experience.

🛡️ Safety Expectations

We take safety seriously, and we ask that all guests do the same. Here is what we expect from everyone on board:

  • Follow all instructions from the captain or crew at all times
  • Wear life jackets when instructed, especially children and non-swimmers
  • No reckless behavior, excessive alcohol consumption, or illegal substances on board
  • Inform the captain of any medical conditions, allergies, or concerns before departure
  • Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times
  • Respect the vessel, equipment, and marine environment

We reserve the right to cancel or end any trip if safety is compromised. No refunds will be issued in such cases.

📝 Self-Operated Rental Waivers

If you choose to rent a boat and operate it yourself, a signed waiver is required before departure. This waiver covers:

  • Acknowledgment of responsibility for the vessel during the rental period
  • Confirmation that you understand basic boat operation and safety procedures
  • Liability release for incidents resulting from operator error or negligence
  • Agreement to return the vessel in the same condition and on time

The waiver is a standard document designed to protect both you and our business. Our team will walk you through it and answer any questions before you sign.

Note: Captain-assisted rentals do not require a waiver, as the captain assumes operational responsibility.

👨‍✈️ Captain Options

You always have the option to add a captain to your rental or trip:

  • Captains are available for an additional fee on any rental
  • Multiple captains are on our team, so availability is flexible
  • Captains handle all navigation, safety, and vessel operation
  • Recommended for unfamiliar waters, larger groups, or anyone who wants to relax

💳 Payment & Booking

Our payment and booking policies are designed to be clear and fair:

  • Full payment is required before departure or vessel pickup
  • We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and cash
  • Bookings are confirmed once payment is received
  • Cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund
  • Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee
  • Weather-related cancellations are handled on a case-by-case basis with full flexibility

🌦️ Weather Policy

Safety always comes first when it comes to weather:

  • We monitor weather conditions closely before and during every trip
  • If conditions are unsafe, we will reschedule or offer a full refund
  • The captain has final authority on weather-related decisions
  • We will always communicate proactively if weather may affect your booking

🌊 Environmental Responsibility

We love the Bahamas and are committed to protecting its waters:

  • No littering or dumping of any kind in the ocean
  • Respect marine life — no touching, feeding, or harassing wildlife
  • Follow designated anchoring and mooring guidelines
  • Help us keep the Bahamas beautiful for future generations

Questions About Our Policies?

We are happy to answer any questions you have about safety, waivers, or policies. Reach out anytime.
